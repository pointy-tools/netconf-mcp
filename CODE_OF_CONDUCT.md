# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

Please be respectful, constructive, and collaborative.

Examples of expected behavior:

- giving helpful technical feedback
- assuming good intent
- being patient with questions and different experience levels
- keeping disagreement focused on the work

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or behavior
- doxxing or exposing private infrastructure details
- bad-faith disruption of discussion or review

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributions that do not support a constructive and safe environment.
